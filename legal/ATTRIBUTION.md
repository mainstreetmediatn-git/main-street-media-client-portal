# Attribution Policy

Last reviewed: 2026-07-03

Main Street Growth OS should present itself as a proprietary Main Street Media Co. product. Attribution is a legal notice obligation, not permission to reuse third-party brands in our UI.

## Attribution Rules

1. Preserve copyright and license notices for any approved third-party code.
2. Keep attribution in legal/about surfaces, not in primary product navigation unless the license requires prominent UI notices.
3. Do not use third-party logos, icons, product names, screenshots, documentation text, marketing claims, or visual identity.
4. Do not imply endorsement, sponsorship, partnership, or compatibility beyond truthful nominative references.
5. Keep source-code comments that are required by license headers.
6. Mark modified third-party files when required by the license.
7. Keep an internal record of source repo, commit SHA, path, license, reviewer, and date for every approved copied file.

## Approved Attribution Entries

No copied source from the audited repositories is currently approved for attribution.

When approved, add entries using this format:

```text
[Project Name]
Source: [repository URL]
Commit/version: [identifier]
License: [license]
Files used: [paths]
Copyright: [copyright line]
Modifications: [none/list]
Notice location: /legal/THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md
Approved by: [name/date]
```
